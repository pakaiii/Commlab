Have you ever wanted to stay living in a single moment?

Sometimes when I’m happy, I get this feeling in my chest that makes it hard to breathe…

And the muscles in my face tense…as if I were about to cry

Because I know the image in front of me will slip between my fingers before I know it. No matter how much I cherish it right now,

it remains fleeting and falls victim to time.

The feeling in my chest is like a panic that I’m losing everything I’ve ever loved to time.

I never want to feel like that again.
